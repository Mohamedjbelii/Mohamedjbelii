## 🌟 Current Project: **TrendPulse**  

### [TrendPulse - Viral Video Automation](https://github.com/mohamedjbelii/trendpulse)
![TrendPulse Logo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Project-Active-brightgreen?style=for-the-badge)
  
🚀 **TrendPulse** is my flagship project, born entirely from my own **idea** and **design**, aimed at automating the upload and management of viral short-form videos across multiple social media platforms.  

### 🛠️ **Key Features**:
- **Multi-platform Uploads**: Automates video uploads to platforms like YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.
- **Viral Trends Detection**: Implements algorithms to identify trending videos for maximum reach.
- **Smart Scheduling**: Enables optimal posting times for better engagement.
- **Platform Analytics**: Tracks performance across platforms to refine strategies.
